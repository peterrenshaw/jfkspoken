June 1946, Massachusetts.
JFK, busy on the campaign trail for the Democratic primary: On the 14th.
Walked the Charlestown neighborhood of Boston for the Bunker Hill Day parade. Collapsed. 
<http://www.dailyjfk.com/days/june-17-1946-monday/> 

On the 16th gave a speech at the Knights Of Columbus Hall, Charlestown, Massachusetts.
<https://www.jfklibrary.org/archives/other-resources/john-f-kennedy-speeches/charlestown-ma-19460616>

On the 18th, won the Democratic primary vote for the 11th Congressional seat in Massachusetts defeating closest riveal Mike Neville by 19426 votes to 10,875.
<http://www.dailyjfk.com/days/june-18-1946/>
